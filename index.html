CorsTech is a civil space agency built in the 1800s. It is a global leader in the field of space exploration with a large number of workforces. Under the administration of Anonymous, the 40th administrator in this agency, CorsTech has made a wide network all over the world. We have worked with many international and commercial partners to explore, discover, and expand knowledge for the benefit of humanity. With an annual budget of over $30 billion in Fiscal Year 2021, CorsTech is continuing to generate more than hundred thousands of jobs, generating more than $100 billion economic output.

We have a total of 38 centers located in some best places all over the world from where researchers can easily explore space and discover new things. We conduct research, testing and development to advance aeronautics. We develop and fund space technologies that will enable future exploration and benefit life on earth.
