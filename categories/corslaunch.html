Step 1 INTERVIEWS

We will host interviews in every country. Registered participants must attend the interview. Though the interview is going to be difficult, it will be based on very basic questions about space. So be prepared.

Step 2 MEDICAL EXAM

Those who pass the interview are will have to take a medical exam in their own country. The centers have been listed here.

Step 3 CONFIRMATION

After you have passed the medical exam too, you will get an email from chors@space.tech for the confirmation of the start of your training session. We would have also sent you an airline ticket with it and some details about what all basic things you would require if you join us.

Step 4 TRAINING SESSIONS

Once you join us in our camp which is located in the Chors campus, you will start with your training session. There will be 10 rounds of training. The first round will be of 1 week and as the number of rounds increases, the duration of the training will increase simultaneously.

Step 5 TEAM FORMATION

The remaining 5 will undergo another medical exam. If any one of the 5 people does not pass the medical exam, he/she will be replaced.
