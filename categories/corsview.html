Corsview

In the Chorsview program, we will invite the students from all over the world to the 3 best places for skygazing. Each place will be located in a different continent. The places would either be a hilly area or an open patch of land where you will be living in tents.

In the camps we will teach you different things that you can see in the sky. There would be moons, planets, stars, etc. We will teach you how to find a constellation, different types of constellations, the black hole, galaxies, etc.

There would be many fun activities, some hunts where you will be given coordinates or hints and you have to find the hidden objects. You will be asked to point out constellations. We will show you different planets, ask you to find one, etc.
